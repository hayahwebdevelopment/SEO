Q3. Some of the benefits of using meta tags are: 
a. SEO Optimization: Meta tags help search engines understand the content of a web page, improving its visibility in search results.
b. Improved Click-Through Rates: Well-crafted meta tags can attract users to click on your link by providing relevant and enticing information.
c. Enhanced User Experience: Meta tags help summarize content accurately, aiding users in quickly determining the relevance of a page to their needs.
d. Social Media Sharing: Meta tags like Open Graph tags enable proper formatting when sharing links on social media platforms, enhancing the visual appeal of shared content.
